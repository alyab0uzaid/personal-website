# Case Study: PrepGuide

PrepGuide is an online platform I co-founded with my brother to provide free resources for SAT preparation.

## Overview

PrepGuide offers accessible, free SAT prep materials to help students prepare without expensive courses or subscriptions.

## Tech Stack

- Webflow for site and CMS
- No-code approach for rapid iteration
